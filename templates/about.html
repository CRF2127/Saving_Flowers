About Me

Hey, I'm Che.

I'm a former web-developer turned sustainability professional. That combination of skill sets makes me lucky enough to participate in projects at that nexus. These projects range from energy management of data-centers to geographic information systems analyses for green vehicles to IoT devices that monitor air quality. Today, I'm a happy civil servant advancing environmental initiatives inside NYC public housing. I also freelance in areas related to web-development, data-analytics, and sustainability initiatives.

This blog is a broad survey of the nexus existing between sustainability and tech.

NY Dept. of Transportation: Last Chance Gas!

Green vehicles like electric cars and biofuel vans may be a tricky purchase. But creating policy incentivizing their investment is even trickier. Pathways to refueling stations, inspections, and much more need to be open. As a consultant, I led a team to scope and gap analysis of all of the steps for the New York State Department of Transportation to green our roads.
